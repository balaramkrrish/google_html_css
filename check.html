Products

ezTask.io : A smart sales automation platform, increases productivity of your sales team by as much as 30% in comparison with other CRMs. Its available in web and native android app.

Features
  • ezTask.io Smart and lightweight sales automation platform
  • Cloud-based sales assistant for field force automation
  • Dedicated Dashboard with real time performance reports to improve your sales numbers.
  • Dynamic pipeline to support customizing workflow.
  • Monitoring target for team and individuals
  • Call recording and analysis to help you in better customer engagement

my2do.in : The native task management application to manage all your tasks, so that you dont need to visit multiple apps.

Features
  • A task can be based on time or location. For location tasks, User will get notification if within specified distance of destination.
  • A task can have multiple participants. Each participant will be notified by mail/sms/push notification about the task. The response from participants will be displayed to Owner of the task.
  • A task can have multiple attachments, group chat.
  • Attendees of the task can chat with each other.
  • Delegate task, Share task status, Share task details for specific period
  • Configurable view setting : Add multiple calendars, task view, enable/disable sms/mail management
  • A collaboration(meeting) task can have location/skype/whatsapp/teleconference as medium to collaborate.
  • eZilly: An AI powered bot to manage all your tasks(Next release).
